Worldwide - Stringency Index

Since Covid-19 has created unprecendeted government action around the world, Oxford Covid-19 Government Response Tracker (OxCGRT) has compiled a dataset that measures the "Stringency" Index. This is calculated based on their 9 response indicators that are listed within the containment and closure policies which consists of school closures or workplace closures scaled to a value from 0 to 100 (100 = strictest). 
More information can be found from their github at https://github.com/OxCGRT/covid-policy-tracker
