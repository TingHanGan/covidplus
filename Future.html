Future of Covid-19
Discover how Covid-19 will potentially continue to grow without taking any action.

By looking at the current trends overall for both the cases and deaths, we can see that it will level off at a constant rate if we were to take no action. That is, a constant rate for cases of 500,000 - 600,000 daily, with a constant rate of ~10,000 deaths per day. 

Thankfully, by taking the necessary actions such as increasing the vaccination rate of the whole world and stricter measures, we are currently at around half of the above expectations. However, this is still a big sum and proper actions still need to be in place before we can call this pandemic to be over. 

NOTE: 
     - The future data presented in the graphs is only a prediction, ONLY IF we were to take no action. 
     - Y-Axis values presented in "Forecase of worldwide new cases" can be translated to the following e.g.,:  2e+05 = 2*10^5 = 200,000 cases.
     - Graphs would ideally be updated once a month to show a greater change from the previous graphs. 

If you cannot view the graphs, please switch to a larger device.
